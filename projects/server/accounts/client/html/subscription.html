What can I do with GoCast that I couldn't before?

With GoCast, you can:

  • Create your own online meeting rooms.
  • Invite others to your rooms to share and collaborate.

How does GoCast help me get more out of my meetings?

In addition to audio/video & text chat, GoCast provides the following shared apps and features:

  • Whiteboard Quickly sketch and share your ideas.
  • Notepad Take notes together.
  • WikiBrowser Browse Wikipedia articles together.
  • FileShare Upload, view & share documents, images and other files.
  • DeskShare Share your screen for presentations (Chrome 26+ only).
  • Persistent Rooms
    • Access your room content anytime, even after your meeting.
    • Prepare your room for a meeting by pre-populating it with relevant content.
