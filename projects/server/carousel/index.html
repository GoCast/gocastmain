Gocast.it plugin was not found

In order to participate you will need the GoCast.it plugin installed.

Please click here Gocast.it.plugin to download. Then click on the downloaded file to install.

Then, after downloading the .tar.gz file, follow these steps to install:

  1. Extract the files with tar xzvf GoCastPlayer_XXX.tar.gz
  2. Go to the directory created by cd GoCastPlayer
  3. Execute the install script ./install.sh
  4. Restart your browser.

... and You are ready to have fun with the GoCast Player.

Login With Facebook

By logging in with Facebook you can invite your friends to your GoCast room. You can post to your wall or send a Facebook message. Your Facebook name will appear as your GoCast screen name. Also, when you turn your webcam off your Facebook profile image will replace your video.

-- OR --
Login Without Facebook

By not logging in with Facebook you can choose your own screen name for the GoCast room. You can invite others to join your room by email. Also, when you turn your webcam off, a blank silhouette will replace your video.
